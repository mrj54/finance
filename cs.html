Borobudur Temple

Step into history at the world's largest Buddhist temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Marvel at intricate stone carvings and witness breathtaking sunrise views over ancient stupas.

Explore the mysterious water castle, once a royal garden of the Sultanate. Discover hidden pools, underground passages, and architectural marvels from the 18th century.
